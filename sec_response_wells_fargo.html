Administrative Proceedings

The links below direct you to the docket pages for adjudicatory proceedings – meaning administrative proceedings adjudicated by the Commission including by its consideration of initial decisions of ALJ and appeals from SRO or PCAOB action – as well as to docket pages for respondents’ requests to vacate bars under Koch v. SEC or Bartko v. SEC. The docket pages contain the Commission opinions and orders as well as significant pleadings for such matters. The links below additionally direct you to the electronic filing system for administrative proceedings and to information for respondents in administrative proceedings.

  • Litigated AP Case Materials (Open)

  • Electronic Filings in Administrative Proceedings (eFAP)
  • Information for Respondents in Administrative Proceedings
  • Subpoena Form to Produce Documents - Proceeding Before the Commission
